Who is Robert Pezzella?

Robert Pezzella is the former school safety director for Worcester Public Schools. Pezzella was a strong proponent of police (SRO) presence in schools. His tenure was marred by accusations that he enforced racially-disproportionate discipline models, i.e punished minority students more severerely than their counterparts.
