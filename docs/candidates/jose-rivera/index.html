Worcester City Council 2025 Election

Jose Rivera

Candidate, D5

Who is Jose Rivera?

Jose Rivera is a former three-time WBA welterweight and super welterweight champion. Rivera has an impressive record, racking up 43 wins over 50 career fights, 25 by knockout. Rivera stands at 5'8" tall and has a 70" reach.

What are Jose Rivera's Political Positions?

  • Police: Rivera is staunchly against "defunding the police", and "will advocate for increased patrols where residents and businesses feel they are needed" (read: increased police presence in Worcester's marginalized communities and non-white neighborhoods). He does not believe city council should have oversight of the police department. source
  • Public Transportation: Rivera has attempted to block protected bike lanes and traffic calming measures on Mill Street, a project that has since reduced automotive speeding and crashes.
