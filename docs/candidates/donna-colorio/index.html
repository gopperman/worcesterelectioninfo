Donna Colorio

Who is Donna Colorio?

Donna Colorio is a former School Committee member and the lone Republican on the City Council. She chairs the Traffic & Parking committee.

What are Donna Colorio's Political Positions?

  • LGBTQ+: Colorio voted against a resolution to declare Worcester a sanctuary for transgender and gender-diverse people. She unsuccessfully tried to water down a resolution to commit $500,000 for a Queer and Trans resilience fund, attempting to replace the word "commit" with "consider".
