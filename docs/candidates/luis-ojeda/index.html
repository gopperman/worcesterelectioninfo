Luis Ojeda

Incumbent, D4

Who is Luis Ojeda?

Luis Ojeda is a lifelong Worcester resident, first-term city councilor, and Assistant Principal at University Park Campus School. Ojeda has worked in education for over 20 years, as a physical education teacher prior to becoming assistant principle. He's involved with several non-profit causes, including Worcester Latino Dollars for Scholars, the Pleasant Street Neighborhood Network Center, 508 Forever Young, and Worcester Coaches 4 Change. On City Council, he chairs the Public Health and Human Services committee.
